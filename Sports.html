Athletics Day 2025:

Athletics Day 2025: This year's championships were for the first time a whole school event, combining the intermediate and senior school. The crowd was buzzing with excitement, cheers, and house chants.Deacon Murray narrowly defeated Theo Panther and Nate Tofilau to claim title to Rosmini’s fastest, winning the 100m. Theo went on to win the 200 and 400m.  In the intermediate age group, Edward Medemblik won the 200 and 400m, Jayden Modigh won the 800 and 1500m and Luke Te Tana won the discus and shot put.  James Boyle stood out in the junior class winning the 100 and 200m. Of note in the Y8 class was Josh Johansen who won the 100, 200, 400 and 800m. Nathan McKearney (Y7) won the long jump, 400 and 200m. Emery won the day for their House 661 points to Liston 655. Curran was third with 574 and Kelly 564 – very close totals for bragging rights. The Rosmini Athletics team will contest the North Harbour title on March 6 and we wish them all the best!
